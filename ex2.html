About us

Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences, formerly Karunya University, is a private residential institute deemed to be university in Coimbatore, India. It was founded by D. G. S. Dhinakaran and his son Paul Dhinakaran.Ranked consecutively for 4 cycles among the Top 100 institutions in India by National Institute Ranking Framework (NIRF) Rankings under the University and Engineering Institution categories.
